# Environment Variables for Netlify Import

Copy everything below and paste into Netlify's "Import environment variables" text box:

```
NODE_ENV=production
FRONTEND_URL=https://temp.netlify.app
JWT_EXPIRES_IN=7d
GEMINI_MODEL=gemini-1.5-flash
ALLOWED_ORIGINS=https://temp.netlify.app
```

Then add these from your .env file (copy values after the = sign):

```
DATABASE_URL=<copy from your .env>
JWT_SECRET=<copy from your .env>
VITE_SUPABASE_URL=<copy from your .env>
VITE_SUPABASE_ANON_KEY=<copy from your .env>
RESEND_API_KEY=<copy from your .env>
RESEND_FROM_EMAIL=<copy from your .env>
ADMIN_EMAIL=erayawellnesstravels@gmail.com
STRIPE_SECRET_KEY=<copy from your .env>
STRIPE_PUBLISHABLE_KEY=<copy from your .env>
STRIPE_WEBHOOK_SECRET=<copy from your .env>
GEMINI_API_KEY=<copy from your .env>
```

Instructions:
1. Open your .env file
2. Replace <copy from your .env> with actual values
3. Paste everything into the text box
4. Check "Contains secret values"
5. Keep "All scopes" and "All deploy contexts" selected
6. Click Import
